Ecommerce Sales & Customer Satisfaction Analysis

📌 Project Overview

Analyzed an ecommerce dataset containing 2,400 transactions over 13 weeks to evaluate sales performance, customer behavior, shipping efficiency, and customer satisfaction.
Used Excel dashboards and data analysis techniques to identify key business insights.

📊 Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

-- Total Orders: 2,400

-- Total Quantity Sold: 11,997 units

-- Total Revenue: $649,020

-- Average Customer Rating: 3.96 / 5

-- Average Delivery Time: 2.34 days

🔍 Analysis Performed

* Sales Trends: Weekly analysis of revenue and quantity sold.

* Customer Behavior: Comparison of App, Website, and Partner platform usage.

* Purchase Patterns: Analysis of number of items per order.

* Product Performance: Identified top products such as T-Shirts, Jeans, and Sneakers.

* Customer Demographics: Gender-based purchase analysis.

* Regional Sales: Sales distribution across different regions of California.

* Shipping Efficiency: Delivery time analysis to identify delays.

Customer Satisfaction: Rating analysis from 1–5 stars.
